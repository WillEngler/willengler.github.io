My name is Will Engler. I'm a junior studying computer science at the University of Pittsburgh. I started this blog to take control of my computer science education. On this blog, you'll find explanations of sample code I've posted on Github, reflections on concepts I'm learning, how-to's on cool topics, and musings about productivity and student life.

If you're a fellow Pitt panther, please shoot me an email or find me at Sennott Square to let me know if my postings help you grok the Pitt curriculum. If you're not a Pitt student, I hope you can still find something interesting here.
